## Ex.No.10
## Content Creation (Reports, Articles, Case Studies, etc.) Using Prompt Patterns

## Date: 20/03/26
## Reg. No.:212223050038

## Aim

To demonstrate the use of various prompt engineering techniques such as Query Decomposition, Decision Making, Answer Engineering, Fact Checklist, Tail Generation, Menu Actions, and Semantic Filtering to generate structured content like articles, reports, and case studies using the AI tool ChatGPT.

The objective of this experiment is to understand how the design of prompts influences the clarity, structure, tone, accuracy, and creativity of AI-generated content.

## Tool Used

The tool used for content generation is ChatGPT, an Artificial Intelligence language model that produces human-like text based on input prompts provided by the user.

Prompts act as instructions that guide the AI model in generating relevant and structured content. The quality of the output depends on how clearly the prompt is designed.

## Introduction

Prompt engineering refers to the process of designing effective inputs to guide AI tools in producing meaningful and accurate outputs.

By modifying the prompt structure, we can:

control content format
improve logical flow
ensure factual accuracy
adjust tone and style
increase depth of explanation

In this experiment, different prompt patterns are applied to generate an Article on Climate Change.

## Selected Example Topic

Article on Climate Change

## Prompt Pattern 1: Query Decomposition
Description

Query decomposition divides a complex topic into smaller sections so that the AI can generate structured content.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Write an article on climate change including:
Introduction
Causes
Effects
Prevention
Conclusion

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI identifies that the topic must be divided into multiple sections.
It processes each keyword as a subtopic and generates content under each heading, improving clarity and organization.

Generated Output (Example)

Introduction
Climate change is a major environmental issue affecting the Earth’s temperature and weather patterns.

Causes
Burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial pollution increase greenhouse gases.

Effects
Climate change results in global warming, floods, droughts, and melting glaciers.

Prevention
Using renewable energy and reducing pollution can control climate change.

Conclusion
Protecting the environment is essential for future generations.

## Prompt Pattern 2: Answer Engineering
Description

Answer engineering specifies how the output should be structured, such as paragraphs, headings, or bullet points.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Write an article on climate change in paragraph format with clear headings.

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI understands that the answer must follow a structured format.
It organizes the content into headings and paragraphs instead of providing unstructured text.

Generated Output (Example)

The AI produces paragraphs under each heading, improving readability and presentation.

## Prompt Pattern 3: Semantic Filter
Description

Semantic filtering controls tone and writing style such as formal, academic, simple, or technical.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Write an article on climate change using formal academic tone.

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI selects professional vocabulary and constructs grammatically formal sentences suitable for academic writing.

Generated Output (Example)

Climate change is a critical environmental challenge caused by increased greenhouse gas emissions due to human activities.

## Prompt Pattern 4: Fact Checklist
Description

Fact checklist ensures the generated content contains correct and meaningful information.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Write an article on climate change including accurate environmental facts and real-world impacts.

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI prioritizes factual information and avoids vague or incorrect statements.

Generated Output (Example)

Climate change causes rising sea levels, melting ice caps, and extreme temperature variations.

## Prompt Pattern 5: Tail Generation
Description

Tail generation extends or continues the content to increase depth and detail.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Extend the article by adding future solutions and technological developments for climate change.

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI continues the existing topic logically, adding additional information without changing context.

Generated Output (Example)

Future solutions include development of renewable energy technologies, electric vehicles, and sustainable environmental policies.

## Prompt Pattern 6: Decision Making Prompt
Description

Decision-making prompts allow AI to choose one option among multiple alternatives.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Choose one topic and generate article:
Climate change
Artificial Intelligence
Renewable Energy

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI selects one topic and generates content based on the selected option.

Generated Output (Example)

AI selects climate change and produces article content.

## Prompt Pattern 7: Menu Action Prompt
Description

Menu action prompts provide multiple format options for content generation.

Prompt Given (Input to AI)

Select one content format and generate:
Report
Article
Story
Topic: Climate Change

How AI Interprets the Prompt

The AI chooses a format and generates content accordingly.

Generated Output (Example)

AI selects article format and produces structured content.

## Procedure

Step 1: Open ChatGPT tool.

Step 2: Enter the basic prompt related to the selected topic.

Step 3: Modify the prompt using different prompt patterns.

Step 4: Observe how each prompt changes the output.

Step 5: Generate refined content using structured prompts.

Step 6: Evaluate content quality based on structure, clarity, and accuracy.

## Final Generated Content

Climate change is a major global environmental problem caused by increased greenhouse gas emissions from human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

Its effects include rising global temperature, melting glaciers, extreme weather conditions, and sea level rise. Climate change negatively affects agriculture, water resources, and biodiversity.

Sustainable solutions include renewable energy adoption, environmental awareness, reducing pollution, and conservation of natural resources.

Collective efforts from governments, industries, and individuals are necessary to reduce climate change impact and protect future generations.

## Evaluation of Prompt Effectiveness

Structured prompts improved the clarity and logical organization of the generated content. Query decomposition helped divide the topic into smaller sections, making the information easier to understand.

Answer engineering improved presentation by organizing content into proper headings and paragraphs.

Semantic filtering ensured that the writing style remained formal and suitable for academic purposes.

Fact checklist prompts helped maintain accuracy and reliability of the information.

Tail generation increased the depth of explanation by extending the content logically.

Decision-making and menu prompts helped guide the AI in selecting the appropriate content type and structure.

Overall, well-designed prompts resulted in more professional and meaningful outputs.

## Result

The experiment demonstrates that prompt engineering techniques significantly improve the quality of AI-generated content. Structured prompts produce more organized, accurate, and readable results compared to simple prompts.

Conclusion

Prompt engineering is an effective method for generating high-quality content using AI tools like ChatGPT. By carefully designing prompts, users can control structure, tone, accuracy, and level of detail in the output.

Different prompt patterns help generate professional articles, reports, and case studies efficiently.

Thus, structured prompting plays an important role in producing meaningful AI-generated content.
